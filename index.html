Adulthood.fyi is a system for reviewing the real experience of being an adult.

Not opinions.
Not advice.
Data.

People rate their experience across the core parts of life, reflect on what’s working (and what isn’t), and see how they compare to everyone else.

The Product

Every review includes the same 7 categories:

Freedom™ independence, decision-making
Relationships™ dating, friendships, marriage
Making Money™ income, stability, pressure
Having a Job™ daily work, burnout, purpose
Purpose & Progress™ direction, growth
Paying for Life™ bills, rent, cost of living
Time & Energy™ fatigue, mental load

Because everyone reviews the same system, patterns emerge:

  • What actually gets better over time
  • What consistently doesn’t
  • How age, location, and life stage shape the experience

This isn’t a complaints box.
It isn’t a gratitude journal.

It’s a structured, honest dataset of what adulthood actually feels like.

Sometimes funny.
Sometimes brutal.
Always real.

Understand adulthood. Don’t just survive it.
